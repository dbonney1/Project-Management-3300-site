Project Scope Management

Project Scope is easy in theory but challenging in practice. It is a measure of the size and complexity of your project. Many resources can be found that you can learn from at your leisure and are free. Corsera.org is a free site for students to learn about Project Management in relatively short informative videos. Podcasts can also a fantastic way to learn about all the distinct aspects of Project Management. Just click on the picture link to begin your learning.
