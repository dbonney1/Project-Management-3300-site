Project Resource Management

Project Resource Management is defined as what you have available to complete a project, i.e., time, money, people, and equipment. This short video gives a brief definition of resource management and going over some cons if any of these resources are not managed or planned out correctly.

Resource Management is not just about identifying the resources you have. It is also about acquiring and managing the resources that are needed for the project. These resources need to be the right resources available to the project manager and the team at the right time and place. This short video explains resource management basics; it has an additional resource link and shows how to utilize resource planning for a project.
