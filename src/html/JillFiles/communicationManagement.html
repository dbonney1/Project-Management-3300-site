Project Communication Management

A project manager must know the communication process, and what is involved to be effective in communicating with the team and the stakeholders. Effective communication uses three processes that ensure information is sent, received, and understood by the appropriate parties. Good communication is essential to thwart any type of miscommunication breakdown that could have a negative impact on the team’s productivity and the final product.

This video gives a summary of Project Communications management and that the project manager should understand the team’s genuine issues and be in touch with the challenges of the project. It is not just about speaking to and hearing from people. It is about understanding the complete message to ensure a successful project.
