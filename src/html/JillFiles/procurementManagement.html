Project Procurement Management

What is Project Procurement management? To procure something is the act of obtaining goods or services. In our case, this refers to a collaboration with external suppliers to get or purchase something for our project purposes. This can be on a large- or small-scale procurement process. A procurement process usually involves several different company areas i.e., purchasing, sales, etc. Project Procurement Management is ultimately all about making a purchase decision and managing it to a successful conclusion.
